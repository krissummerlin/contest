How it works


To get started you'll need a link that's unique to only you. Sign up at cleantriad.com by entering your email.

You'll see several sharing options, like, facebook send, tweet, linked in share, and email. Each option already has your special link applied. Optionally, you can copy the link and share it any way you like.

To see who's generating the most points, just go to cleantriad.com/leaders and you can see who's gaining the most points.
